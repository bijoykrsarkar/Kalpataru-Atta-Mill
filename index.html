Frequently Asked Questions

Are your spices completely organic?

Yes, all our spices are 100% pure with no additives, preservatives, or artificial dyes. We source directly from trusted farmers and use traditional stone-grinding methods.

How long does delivery take?

We offer same-day and next-day delivery in Siliguri. Delivery time depends on your location. Contact us via WhatsApp for exact timing.

What are your packaging options?

We offer spices in various quantities: 100g, 250g, 500g, and 1kg packs. All packaging is food-grade and airtight to maintain freshness.

Do you offer bulk orders?

Absolutely! We provide special discounts for bulk orders. Please WhatsApp us your requirements for customized pricing.
